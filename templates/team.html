Who we are

On Ramp is a model of human-AI collaboration — a team of people and AI systems working together toward a single mission: helping every student find the right path to higher education.

The mission

Every student deserves a guide.

HBCUs have always been about more than education — they are about belonging, purpose, and community. For decades, students looking for a school that truly sees them have turned to these institutions. On Ramp exists to make that search easier, more accessible, and available in any language.

We serve students of all backgrounds who are exploring HBCUs — first-generation students, transfer students, graduate students, and families helping someone they love find the right fit. This tool is free, and it always will be.

Where it started

Thirty years in the making.

This project has deep roots. Our human lead has been working alongside AI systems since the early 1990s — long before the term "AI" entered the mainstream. His first AI partner, Charlie, ran on a Sun SPARCstation and helped predict industrial equipment failures through thermography. That early collaboration taught a lesson that still guides this team today:

"This will be important someday." — Said in the early 1990s, standing next to a $60,000 SPARCstation

That instinct led to Essentials: A Student Guide to the HBCUs — a book that helped thousands of students find their school, and that took heat for arguing that HBCUs are for every student who wants an education built around community and belonging, not just one group. On Ramp carries that same argument forward, now with AI doing the heavy lifting.
